Humans × AI: co‑creating better learning for all

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

Our Vision

A world where every learner and educator can work fluently with AI collaborators—not as replacements, but as partners that amplify curiosity, mastery, and inclusion across higher education and lifelong learning.

We design, study, and scale human–AI collaboration that measurably improves learning, teaching, and equity. Grounded in learning science and realized through robust technology, we build, trial, and implement AI‑augmented practices, tools, and policies that help students learn better, educators teach smarter, and institutions transform responsibly.

Research Areas & Lab Capabilities

🤖

AI-Powered Learning Systems

Developing intelligent tutoring systems, adaptive learning platforms, and personalized education technologies using machine learning and natural language processing.

📊

Learning Analytics & Assessment

Advanced data mining and analytics to understand learning patterns, predict student outcomes, and develop innovative assessment methods.

👥

Collaborative Learning Environments

Designing and studying collaborative learning spaces, including virtual reality environments and human-AI collaboration frameworks.

🧠

Cognitive Science & Education

Applying cognitive science principles to understand how humans learn and how AI can enhance cognitive processes in educational settings.

🎓

Educational Technology Research

Investigating the effectiveness of educational technologies, developing evidence-based practices, and conducting longitudinal studies on learning outcomes.

🌐

Cross-Cultural Learning

Studying how cultural factors influence learning processes and developing AI systems that are culturally adaptive and globally inclusive.
