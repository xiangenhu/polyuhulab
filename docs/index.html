Our Vision

A world where every learner and educator can work fluently with AI collaborators—not as replacements, but as partners that amplify curiosity, mastery, and inclusion across higher education and lifelong learning.

We design, study, and scale human–AI collaboration that measurably improves learning, teaching, and equity. Grounded in learning science and realized through robust technology, we build, trial, and implement AI‑augmented practices, tools, and policies that help students learn better, educators teach smarter, and institutions transform responsibly.

Research Focus Areas

Human–AI Co‑Inquiry & Co‑Creation

What new forms of inquiry, writing, design, and problem‑solving emerge when learners treat AI as a teammate?

  • Scaffolding metacognition and self‑explanation with AI dialogue
  • Collaborative writing/coding/design workflows
  • Classroom knowledge‑building with AI participation

AI Orchestration for Educators

How do instructors plan, enact, and reflect with AI support—without losing professional judgment?

  • Lesson‑planning copilots aligned to learning outcomes
  • Real‑time orchestration dashboards
  • Co‑assessment and explainable feedback loops

Assessment & Integrity in AI‑Rich Learning

How can assessment remain authentic and fair when AI is everywhere?

  • Process‑based assessment and reflection traces
  • Transform "cheating risk" into learning opportunity
  • Calibrated, explainable AI feedback systems

Multimodal Learning Analytics

What signals predict productive vs. unproductive human–AI collaboration?

  • Text, clickstream, and interaction log analysis
  • Detecting over‑reliance vs. productive struggle
  • Nudging toward mastery and autonomy

Equity & Accessibility

How does AI collaboration close—not widen—opportunity gaps?

  • Multilingual copilot design
  • Culturally responsive prompts
  • Accessibility‑first interaction models

Ethics, Governance & Policy

What workable policies help institutions adopt AI responsibly?

  • Risk assessment and model cards
  • Data governance and audit checklists
  • Transparent AI‑use policies
